Changelog

What each version brings. The app updates itself from Settings → Update — here you can see what you'll get.

v1.8.0

July 20, 2026
  • Big internal cleanup ahead of launch: lighter install package and a simpler, more reliable voice engine.
  • Documentation and website corrected and brought up to date.

v1.7.8

July 20, 2026
  • Fixed: the "Voice commands" guide in Settings didn't appear in some cases; it now always shows.

v1.7.5 – v1.7.7

July 18-19, 2026
  • More robust voice: fixed the microphone on iPhone, voice errors now visible on screen, and a command guide available in Settings.
  • More system apps: the detector now also finds apps like the file manager or the terminal, so you can launch them from your phone.
  • The phone app now picks up updates automatically on every release.

v1.7.0 – v1.7.4

July 14-18, 2026
  • Firefox as the TV browser: more compatible, with ad blocking (uBlock Origin) included automatically.
  • Real-time latency indicator in the status bar.
  • More robust installation (no certificate hangs) and a fail-safe auto-updater.
  • New installer option to keep the TV screen from turning off on its own.
  • Design fixes for iPhone (16:9 screens, icons, notch).

v1.6.0

July 15, 2026
  • Spanish keyboards: support for ES and LATAM layouts (ñ, accents and AltGr symbols).
  • The Home button now truly leaves a clean desktop, and "back" navigates without rewinding the video.
  • Fewer latency spikes in the remote.

v1.5.0 and earlier

July 2026
  • "Cozy Media", the app's design language, is born.
  • 6-digit PIN pairing, TV status panel, welcome tour, built-in feedback form and the theme system.
  • Dedicated support for KDE Plasma Bigscreen.
